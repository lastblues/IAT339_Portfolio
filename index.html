PROJECTS

You will find the list of different projects I have worked on over my undergraduate career. Projects vary from academic to personal project and they showcase my skills in creating multimedia content.

Corporate Identity Designer: Fukui Byora

I had the opportunity in the fall of 2014 to take part in the international Co-op program in Japan. I was hired by Fukui Byora Ltd., as their corporate identity designer. Fukui Byora is a cold heading specialist located in Fukui, Japan. Fukui Byora create heading parts such as fasteners for automotive, electronics and medical products across the world. My tasks included creating content for their newly launched website and promotional videos for advertisements and exhibitions.

Be Human: A League of Legends Animation

"Be Human" was the final project of the computer animation course at SFU (IAT 437) course. "Be Human" is a 3D adaptation of a League of Legends comic of the same title. The story features two robot characters, "Orianna" and "Blitcrank", where they question what it means to be a human.
